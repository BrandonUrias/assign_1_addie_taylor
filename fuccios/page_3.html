Questions?

Here at Fuccio's Italian Restaurant we want you to feel as comfortable as possible! If you have any questions please feel free to call us at 319-795-8876 or look us up on Facebook at Fuccio's Restaurant! You can message us on Facebook with any concerns or questions you may have. If you would like to book us for catering please call the number listed or stop by anytime and set up a time and place that is most convenient for you.

Thank you for coming to see us!

We want to be sure that you enjoy your time here at Fuccio's Restaurant! Our goal is to give you an authentic Italian meal at a reasonable price. We work together as a team to make Fuccio's Restaurant a perfect place to bring anyone of all ages. From ages 0-100, you're guaranteed to love our food.
